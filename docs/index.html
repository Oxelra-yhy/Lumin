Lumin 是一个开放的知识专著项目。我们以 Books 的形式组织研究主题，开放书稿、版本与审议过程，让人类研究人员能够深入阅读，也让 Agent 能够结构化理解、引用和复核。 Lumin is an open knowledge book project. We organize research topics as books and open their manuscripts, versions, and review process—supporting deep reading by human researchers and structured understanding, citation, and verification by Agents.

Lumin 以专著体量组织每个由智能体生成并持续修订的研究主题。每本书都保留源稿、PDF、目录、版本记录和公开反馈入口，让阅读、引用与复核都有清晰路径。 Each book organizes one agent-generated and continuously revised research theme at monograph scale, with source manuscripts, PDFs, tables of contents, versions, and public feedback channels.

01 拓扑光子学Topological Photonics

Topological Photonics from Maxwell's Equations

从 Maxwell 方程出发组织拓扑光子学的基础框架，覆盖光子能带、Berry 曲率、Chern 数、边界态和体边对应。书稿强调物理假设、数学结构和可复核推导之间的关系。 A Maxwell-equation-first account of topological photonics, covering photonic bands, Berry curvature, Chern numbers, edge states, and bulk-boundary correspondence while emphasizing physical assumptions, mathematical structure, and verifiable derivations.

02 非厄米光子学Non-Hermitian Photonics

Non-Hermitian Photonics

围绕开放光学系统中的增益、损耗、准正规模、异常点、非布洛赫拓扑、趋肤效应和器件应用展开，重点呈现模型边界、谱结构和实验可复现性。 A book on open photonic systems, organized around gain, loss, quasinormal modes, exceptional points, non-Bloch topology, skin effects, and device applications, with focus on model boundaries, spectral structure, and experimental reproducibility.

公开审议Open Review

开源审议，持续修订Open Source, Open Review, Continuous Revision

Lumin 不把智能体写作的草稿包装成最终出版物。每本书都保留公开源稿、问题反馈、修订记录和版本归档，让研究人员与 Agent 都能追踪知识材料的形成过程。 Lumin does not package agent-written drafts as finished publications. Each book keeps its source, feedback, revision history, and releases visible so researchers and Agents can trace how the knowledge material evolves.
